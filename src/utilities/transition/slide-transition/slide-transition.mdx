# Slide transition
A slide transition refers to the animation effect applied to elements when they change position. It gives the illusion of sliding or moving smoothly from one position to another.

## How to use
To use ```slide``` transition, just surround your component with ```Transition``` <a href="https://vuejs.org/guide/built-ins/transition.html" target="_blank">Vue build-in component</a> with attribute ```name="slide"```
